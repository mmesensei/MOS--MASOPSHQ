MOS FOUNDING LIFETIME ACCESS & PRIVACY CHARTER
================================================

1. LIFETIME ENTITLEMENT
The Owner account and the first 100 eligible Founding VIP accounts hold a
permanent, non-expiring "Founding Lifetime VIP" entitlement. This is an
access right, not a recurring subscription. It does not expire, renew, or
require payment while MOS continues to operate. UI must display it as
"Founding Lifetime VIP" or "Lifetime Founder" and MUST NEVER show an
expiration date.

2. PROTECTED LIFETIME ACCESS
Founding Lifetime access must remain active indefinitely and MUST NOT be
removed by pricing changes, plan changes, product releases, updates,
migrations, ownership restructuring, feature additions, billing changes,
membership restructuring, redesigns, version upgrades, or new tiers.
Future tiers must not reduce or replace Founding Lifetime benefits.

3. ROLE PROTECTION (PERMISSION ISOLATION)
Founding Lifetime VIP is stored as a profile flag, NEVER as a role in
public.user_roles. Founding Lifetime users are premium end-users only and
MUST NEVER be granted: Owner, Administrator, Developer, database, security
admin, source-code, internal-tools, system-config, or user-management
capabilities. Owner role remains isolated to the owner account.

4. SECURITY
- Entitlement fields (is_founding_vip, founding_vip_number, vip_status,
  vip_granted_at, vip_revoked_at, subscription_tier, is_internal_account,
  fraud_flag, owner_notes) are protected by a BEFORE UPDATE trigger:
  only the owner (verified via is_owner(auth.uid())) can change them.
- Assignment is a SECURITY DEFINER function triggered by
  auth.users.email_confirmed_at (verified email only), with row-level
  locking on founding_vip_config to guarantee at most 100 slots.
- Never trust frontend, localStorage, cookies, or client requests for
  lifetime status. All checks flow through RLS + server functions.
- Every grant, revocation, restore, fraud flag, manual grant, and
  promotion-state change writes an immutable row to founding_vip_audit.

5. PRIVACY
User information is never sold or shared except to operate the service or
comply with applicable law. Protections in force: secure authentication,
principle of least privilege, row-level security on all user-scoped
tables, comprehensive audit logging, and protection against privilege
escalation (roles live in a separate table checked by SECURITY DEFINER
has_role()).

6. ACCOUNT RECOVERY
Lifetime access survives password resets, email updates, device changes,
platform updates, and database migrations. Compromised accounts may be
temporarily suspended (owner_revoke_vip with reason) and MUST be restored
(owner_restore_vip) once ownership is verified.

7. EXCEPTIONAL CIRCUMSTANCES (OWNER-ONLY)
Revocation is reserved for: user-requested deletion, permanent compromise
requiring migration, or a binding legal order. Every such action requires
the owner and writes an immutable audit record via founding_vip_audit.

8. TESTING INVARIANTS
- Owner account always retains Owner role AND Lifetime entitlement.
- The first 100 email-verified, non-internal, non-admin accounts receive
  a permanent Founding Lifetime VIP number in [1..100].
- Lifetime status persists across deploys, upgrades, and migrations.
- Users cannot self-modify their role or entitlement fields.
- Founding Lifetime users receive all premium features and NO
  administrative permissions.
